Instructions

For training the model, use only the training set. Do not use validation and test sets.

The organizers request that each team upload their final or updated results to the competition website by 5th August 2024.

The team member can upload the results upto 10 times

For each submission, teams must provide the transcriptions of their developed solutions against the provided test sets, along with a brief description of their solution. Submitting the trained model and code is optional but highly encouraged

Please note that all submissions will be verified by the organizers, and the results will only reflect at the end of the day.
